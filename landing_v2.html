FAQs

Who can apply for a Sensible Savings Fixed Term Bond?
To be eligible, you must be a UK resident over the age of 18, with an existing UK current bank account. Applications can be made individually or jointly with one other person. These rates are not available to companies, charities, clubs or associations.
How do I deposit funds?
You can deposit funds into your new Fixed Bond account by completing the final step of the online application with us and using your bank's internet banking facility to make a transfer. Alternatively, you can mail a cheque with a postal application.
Will I be able to access my funds prior to the maturity of the Bond?
No - you cannot access funds until the Bond matures. You can decide before the maturity date whether you wish for your deposit plus accrued interest to be paid into the bank account specified in your application, or to re-invest the funds in a new Bond at the applicable rate.
